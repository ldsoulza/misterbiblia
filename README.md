### About

This repository hosts the legal documentation website for **Mister Bible** (also known as *Mister Biblia* / *Mister Bíblia*), a biblical trivia and minigames app available on Android and iOS.

The site is deployed via [GitHub Pages](https://pages.github.com/) and serves as the public-facing legal information center required by Google Play, the App Store, AdMob, and other services.

---
