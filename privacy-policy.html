Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Mister Bible ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application, available on Android and iOS.

1. Data Collection

This app may use Third-Party Authentication (Google, Apple, Microsoft, or Facebook), as per your choice, only to provide secure access to your data. We access your email address and a unique identifier provided by the authentication provider to ensure only you can access your account.

You may also use the app anonymously without providing any personal information. Anonymous users have limited functionality (local-only progress).

2. Purpose of Data Collection

We use your email to securely link your app progress and achievements to a cloud backup via Supabase, allowing you to recover your data on any device. We do not collect, sell, or use your personal information for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

The app integrates with the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — Authentication and cloud data storage. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob — Advertising. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve ads. Google Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat — Subscription and in-app purchase management. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Services / Sign-In — Authentication on Android devices. Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple Sign-In — Authentication on iOS devices. Apple Privacy Policy

4. Data Storage and Retention

Your progress, achievements, and app usage profile are stored in the cloud via Supabase for backup and optimization purposes. Local data is also stored on your device using SharedPreferences for offline access and performance.

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, all associated data is permanently removed from our servers.

5. Advertising

The app displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may use cookies, device identifiers, and other technologies to serve personalized or non-personalized ads based on your preferences and applicable regulations. You may opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings.

6. Children's Privacy

Mister Bible is an educational app suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can promptly delete it.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time via the app settings.
  • Opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
